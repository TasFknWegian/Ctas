About Us

We’ve spent over 10 years helping Tasmanians choose, maintain and enjoy their caravans. From first vans to long-haul tourers, we focus on practical advice, solid workmanship and support that lasts well beyond delivery.

We’re customer-satisfaction driven — clear communication, fair quoting and quality work we stand behind.

  • 10+ years in the caravan industry with a Launceston-based workshop & showroom.
  • All-brand servicing & repairs — brakes & bearings, 12V/240V, plumbing, chassis & suspension.
  • Specialists in New Age & Highclere models and aftercare.
  • Transparent process — clear quotes, timely updates and every van test-checked before handover.
  • Local & independent — proudly serving Tasmanian travellers.
