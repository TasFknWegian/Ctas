Services

When it comes to keeping your caravan in tip-top shape, a service at the Caravans Tasmania Service Department delivers the assurance and peace of mind you’re looking for. We cater for all caravan brands, and our professionally trained service team has a wealth of knowledge and experience.

Trust your dream in safe hands at our service centre based in Launceston, Tasmania.

About Us

We’ve spent over 10 years helping Tasmanians choose, maintain and enjoy their caravans. From first vans to long-haul tourers, we focus on practical advice, solid workmanship and support that lasts well beyond delivery.

We’re customer-satisfaction driven — clear communication, fair quoting and quality work we stand behind.
